Submissions

Submit to FemiNICHE

FemiNICHE is an online magazine covering pop culture, criticism, and creative writing. We publish essays, reviews, cultural commentary, fiction, and poetry by women and gender-diverse people. We are currently open for submissions for Summer 2026.

Who Can Submit

FemiNICHE welcomes submissions from women and gender-diverse people at any stage of their writing journey. First-time contributors are encouraged to pitch.

What We're Looking For

Essays

Longform personal or cultural essays with a strong voice and a clear argument.

800 – 3,000 words

Reviews

Film, TV, music, books, or anything worth having an opinion about.

400 – 900 words

The Discourse

Hot takes, cultural commentary, and responses to things currently happening. Punchy and opinionated.

300 – 1,000 words

Obsessions

Short, fun recommendations: something you've been watching, reading, or listening to that you can't stop thinking about.

150 – 300 words

Fiction

Short literary fiction with a strong sense of voice and world. Any subgenre welcome.

Up to 6,000 words

Poetry

Any form. Submit up to 3 poems per submission in one document.

Up to 3 poems

Guidelines

  • We accept previously unpublished work only.
  • We are fine with simultaneous submissions but please notify us immediately if your work is accepted elsewhere.
  • Submit as a Word document or Google Doc link to feminichemag@gmail.com with the subject line: SUBMISSION — [Category] — [Title].
  • Include a 2–3 sentence bio in the body of your email.
  • We aim to respond within 4–6 weeks. If you haven't heard from us after 6 weeks, feel free to follow up.
  • Contributors are not paid at this time. We hope to change this as FemiNICHE grows.

A Note on Editing

We reserve the right to lightly edit accepted work for clarity and style. If you are unhappy with any changes made before publication, reach out and we will work it out together.

Copyright & AI

We take plagiarism seriously and will not publish work that is not your own. This includes work generated by or substantially assisted by AI.
